Glossary

TermDefinition
EpochA batch processing window in which multiple customer deposits or redemptions are collected and settled together. Epochs align on-chain settlement with off-chain RWA investment cycles.
ShareA token representing a customer's proportional ownership in the vault. Shares are minted when deposits are settled and burned when redemptions are settled.
NAVNet Asset Value. The total value of the vault's underlying RWA portfolio. Used to calculate the share price.
Share PriceThe current value of one share, calculated as NAV divided by total shares outstanding. Updated daily based on the underlying portfolio performance.
CuratorThe entity responsible for managing the vault's epoch lifecycle — freezing batches, executing settlements, and updating the NAV.
PlatformThe fintech application that integrates TBook Yield Layer to offer yield to its customers. Platforms control the user experience and signing keys.
CustomerAn end user of the platform who deposits USDC to earn yield or redeems shares to withdraw USDC.
Lazy SettlementAn optimization where a customer's pending deposit or redemption from a previous epoch is automatically settled on their next interaction with the vault, requiring no additional transactions.
USDCA dollar-pegged stablecoin used as the deposit and withdrawal currency in TBook vaults.
