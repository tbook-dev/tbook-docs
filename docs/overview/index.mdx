# What is TBook Yield Layer

TBook Yield Layer is turnkey infrastructure that enables fintechs to offer RWA-backed yield to their customers — without building or managing any of the underlying investment infrastructure.

Your platform integrates once via SDK or REST API. Your customers deposit USDC, earn yield from real-world assets, and redeem anytime. You stay in control of the user experience and signing keys.

## Current Product

**rcUSDP Vault** — A US Treasury-backed Money Market Fund vault offering approximately 5% APY. All deposits are allocated to short-duration US Treasury Bills, providing institutional-grade yield with daily NAV updates.

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Non-custodial** | Your platform controls signing keys. TBook never holds customer funds directly. |
| **T+1 Settlement** | Deposits and redemptions settle within 1-2 business days, similar to traditional finance. |
| **Multisig Protected** | All vault operations require 2-of-3 multisig approval. |
| **Third-party Audited** | Smart contracts audited by ScaleBit/MoveBit. |
| **On-chain Verifiable** | All vault state, share balances, and settlements are verifiable on-chain. |

## How It Works

1. **Customers deposit USDC** via your platform's interface
2. **Multisig approves** batch settlement (2-of-3 signers)
3. **USDC is allocated** to RWA strategies (T-Bills, private credit, and more)
4. **Yield accrues daily** as the NAV appreciates automatically
5. **Redeem anytime** with T+1 settlement — USDC is returned to customers
