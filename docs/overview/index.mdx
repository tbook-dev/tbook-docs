# What is TBook Yield Layer

TBook Yield Layer is turnkey infrastructure that enables fintechs to offer RWA-backed yield to their customers — without building or managing any of the underlying investment infrastructure.

Your platform integrates once via SDK or REST API. Your customers deposit USDC, earn yield from real-world assets, and redeem anytime. You stay in control of the user experience and signing keys.

## Multi-Strategy Architecture

TBook Yield Layer supports multiple RWA strategies — each strategy is deployed as an independent **Vault**. Your platform selects which vaults to offer based on your product needs and risk appetite.

Available strategy types include:

- **US Treasury Bills** — Short-duration government securities, institutional-grade yield
- **Private Credit** — Higher-yield lending strategies with diversified credit exposure
- **Gold** — Commodity-backed vaults for inflation hedging
- **Additional strategies** — New RWA categories are added as the platform expands

Each vault operates independently with its own epoch lifecycle, NAV oracle, and settlement cadence. This isolation ensures that risk is contained and settlement timing matches the underlying asset class.

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Non-custodial** | Your platform controls signing keys. TBook never holds customer funds directly. |
| **Batch Settlement** | Deposits and redemptions are processed in batches (epochs), aligning on-chain settlement with off-chain RWA investment cycles. Settlement timing depends on the underlying asset. |
| **Multisig Protected** | All vault operations require 2-of-3 multisig approval. |
| **Third-party Audited** | Smart contracts audited by ScaleBit/MoveBit. |
| **On-chain Verifiable** | All vault state, share balances, and settlements are verifiable on-chain. |

## How It Works

1. **Customers deposit USDC** via your platform's interface
2. **Multisig approves** batch settlement (2-of-3 signers)
3. **USDC is allocated** to the vault's RWA strategy (T-Bills, private credit, gold, and more)
4. **Yield accrues** as the NAV appreciates — reflected automatically in share price
5. **Redeem anytime** — batch settlement processes redemptions and returns USDC to customers
